Our Story

"I'm a gym girly myself — and I could never find anything that was modest, cute, and actually worked."

Whether I was at the gym, in a Pilates class, boxing, or just on a walk — I always had the same problem. Everything was either too revealing, too frumpy, or made me choose between looking good and feeling covered.

I wanted long sleeves that didn't overheat. High waists that stayed up. Fabrics that moved with me and looked just as good off the mat. I searched everywhere and couldn't find it, so I made it myself.

MÓDE is for every woman who's ever felt like activewear wasn't made for her. It was made for us.

Khadidja Founder, MÓDE
